About Pinus Hockey

We are a beer league division C1 hockey team out of Austin, Texas. Founded in 2016 by Adam Darrenkamp and Alexis Schrubbe. Since being founded, Pinus has played in 3 division championship games, losing all 3.

Pinus is the Genus name for all members of the pine tree. Pines are a softwood tree that maintains its foilage throughout the year.
